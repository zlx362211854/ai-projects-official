# NovelForge English Showcase Video Design

## Style Prompt

Premium cinematic AI writing studio film. Dark editorial canvas, cool cyan accents, ivory typography, and real product footage framed as a high-end product showcase. The language should be English-only for the English website state.

## Colors

- Background black: `#08090B`
- Ivory text: `#F5F1E8`
- Cyan accent: `#8BE9E2`
- Antique gold: `#D7B76A`
- Panel black: `#111419`

## Typography

- Display: `Georgia`
- UI labels: `Montserrat`
- Technical labels: `IBM Plex Mono`

## What NOT to Do

- No Chinese text in the English output.
- No generic AI neon-purple treatment.
- No unreadable captions.
- No jump cuts.
