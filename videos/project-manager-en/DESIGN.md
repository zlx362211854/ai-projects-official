# AI Project Manager Showcase Video Design

## Style Prompt

Premium enterprise AI workflow film. Dark glass, antique gold, copper highlights, precise interface overlays, and calm but confident product motion. The piece should feel like a polished official-site hero video for a serious project planning system.

## Colors

- Background black: `#08090B`
- Ivory text: `#F5F1E8`
- Antique gold: `#D7B76A`
- Copper accent: `#D9874F`
- Muted panel: `#17191D`

## Typography

- Display: `Georgia`
- UI labels: `Montserrat`
- Technical labels: `IBM Plex Mono`

## What NOT to Do

- No neon-purple AI palette.
- No generic chatbot bubbles.
- No unreadable small labels.
- No jump cuts.
