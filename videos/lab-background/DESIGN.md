# AI Product Lab Background Film Design

## Style Prompt

Premium dark technology with editorial restraint: a black-glass product film for two serious AI systems. The visual language combines Velvet Standard luxury spacing with Data Drift motion, but avoids the common purple-neon AI look. The video should feel like a keynote opener for an independent AI product lab.

## Colors

- Background black: `#08090B`
- Warm ivory text: `#F5F1E8`
- Antique gold accent: `#D7B76A`
- Copper project accent: `#D9874F`
- Cool cyan project accent: `#8BE9E2`

## Typography

- Display: `Georgia`, used large and calm for the premium editorial voice.
- Data and labels: `Montserrat`, with tabular numerals and uppercase tracking.
- Technical tags: `IBM Plex Mono`, for graph and architecture labels.

## Motion

- Slow parallax, orbital lines, luminous nodes, and deliberate text reveals.
- Scene transitions use dark focus-pull / blur crossfade covers.
- Motion should feel expensive, not frantic.

## What NOT to Do

- No purple-blue gradient hero look.
- No generic SaaS cards or repeated equal tiles.
- No jump cuts.
- No tiny unreadable body text.
- No busy particle soup that competes with the website foreground.
